About Us

This is a Entrepreneur cell. Join the wave of disruptive innovations

An Entrepreneurship Development Cell (Antha Prerna) is functioning in the college with the intention of nurturing entrepreneurship skills of the students. The cell provides a platform for the students to pursue entrepreneurial activities and also provide assistance to potential entrepreneurs.

Mission

The mission of cell is to facilitate the education and development of students

Vision

To provide a nurturing ground for entrepreneurs and new business development initiatives.

Activities

To provide a nurturing ground for entrepreneurs and new business development initiatives.Enlarging the small and medium enterprise sectors, which offer better potential for employment generation.

This Entrepreneur Cell has been initiated by Ramanujan College, University Of College.
